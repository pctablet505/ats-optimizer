Senior Computer Vision Engineer — Perception & AI

We are hiring a Senior Computer Vision Engineer to build perception systems for our next-generation smart device platform.

Responsibilities:
- Design and implement computer vision algorithms for object detection, segmentation, tracking, and recognition
- Develop synthetic data generation pipelines to address real-world data scarcity for edge-case scenarios
- Build and train models for face recognition, scene understanding, and visual anomaly detection
- Optimize CV models for deployment on mobile/embedded hardware (Android, ARM, NPU)
- Implement image restoration and enhancement pipelines (denoising, super-resolution, inpainting)
- Collaborate with hardware teams on DSP/NPU-aware model design and quantization
- Build datasets and evaluation frameworks; push accuracy from baseline to production-grade

Requirements:
- 3+ years experience in computer vision and deep learning
- Strong knowledge of PyTorch or TensorFlow; experience with OpenCV
- Hands-on experience with detection frameworks (YOLO, Faster R-CNN, DETR)
- Experience with segmentation models (Mask R-CNN, SAM, U-Net)
- Knowledge of face recognition systems, biometric authentication pipelines
- Experience deploying models on Android (TFLite, ONNX, CoreML)
- Proficient in Python; C++ a strong plus for performance-critical components
- Understanding of model compression: pruning, quantization, knowledge distillation

Nice to have:
- Experience with generative models for synthetic data (GANs, Diffusion Models)
- Background in image inpainting or restoration (shadow removal, deblurring)
- Research publication or patent in computer vision
- Experience with multi-sensor fusion (IMU + camera)
