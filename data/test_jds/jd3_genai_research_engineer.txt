GenAI Research Engineer — Foundation Models

Join our Foundation Models team to push the boundaries of generative AI. You will work at the intersection of research and engineering, taking cutting-edge models from paper to production.

Responsibilities:
- Implement and fine-tune large language models (LLMs) and multimodal models using PyTorch and JAX
- Apply parameter-efficient fine-tuning methods: LoRA, QLoRA, adapters, prefix tuning
- Develop and evaluate diffusion-based image and video generation pipelines
- Build training infrastructure for distributed fine-tuning on GPU/TPU clusters
- Optimize inference performance: speculative decoding, continuous batching, KV cache management
- Conduct ablation studies and systematic experiments; publish findings internally and externally
- Collaborate with product teams to integrate GenAI capabilities into user-facing features
- Contribute to open-source GenAI ecosystem (Hugging Face, Keras, LangChain)

Requirements:
- MS or PhD in Machine Learning, Computer Science, or related field (or equivalent experience)
- 2+ years hands-on experience with LLM training or fine-tuning
- Deep understanding of transformer architectures: attention, positional encoding, layer norm
- Experience with JAX, PyTorch, or TensorFlow at scale
- Knowledge of efficient training techniques: gradient checkpointing, mixed precision, FSDP/DeepSpeed
- Familiarity with RLHF, DPO, or other alignment methods
- Strong Python programming; experience with CUDA or XLA a plus

Nice to have:
- Experience with multimodal models (image+text, audio+text)
- Contributions to open-source ML projects (Keras, Hugging Face Transformers)
- Experience with Gemma, Llama, or Mistral model families
- Background in reinforcement learning applied to sequential decision-making
