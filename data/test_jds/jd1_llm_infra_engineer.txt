Senior LLM Infrastructure Engineer — AI Platform Team

We are building the next generation of LLM serving infrastructure and are looking for a Senior LLM Infrastructure Engineer to join our AI Platform team.

Responsibilities:
- Design and implement scalable LLM inference pipelines supporting 100B+ parameter models
- Optimize model serving latency and throughput using quantization (INT4/INT8/FP16), Flash Attention, and custom CUDA kernels
- Build and maintain model export pipelines for deployment across GPU/TPU clusters and edge hardware
- Implement benchmarking frameworks to measure tokens/sec, TTFT, and memory utilization across hardware configurations
- Collaborate with research teams to productionize models (Llama, Gemma, Mistral, GPT variants)
- Work with JAX, PyTorch, and TensorFlow backends in a multi-framework environment
- Contribute to open-source model hubs and inference runtimes (vLLM, TGI, TFLite/LiteRT)

Requirements:
- 3+ years of experience in ML engineering with focus on large model inference or training infrastructure
- Strong proficiency in Python and C++
- Hands-on experience with model quantization (QAT, PTQ, GPTQ, AWQ)
- Experience with distributed training/serving frameworks (JAX, DeepSpeed, Megatron)
- Familiarity with transformer architectures (attention mechanisms, KV cache, rotary embeddings)
- Experience with GPU/TPU optimization (profiling, memory management, kernel fusion)
- Knowledge of Flash Attention, LoRA/QLoRA, and efficient fine-tuning techniques

Nice to have:
- Experience with LiteRT/TFLite or other edge inference runtimes
- Open-source contributions to ML frameworks (Keras, PyTorch, JAX)
- Experience with multi-modal models (vision-language models)
